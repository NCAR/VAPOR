Non CF-Compliant NetCDF          
-----------------------

If your NetCDF data does not follow `CF Conventions <https://cfconventions.org/>`_, you can add *Coordainte Variables* and *Variable Attributes* to your files to make it do so.

See examples on how to achieve this :ref:`here <ncdfExamples>`.

If you have trouble, contact us `on our forum <https://vapor.discourse.group/>`_.
